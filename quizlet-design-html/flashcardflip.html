Ephemeral

Definition: Lasting for a very short time. Origin: Greek 'ephemeros' (lasting a day). Usage: 'The beauty of a sunset is ephemeral.'

Etymology: From Greek 'ephemeros' meaning 'lasting only one day,' referring to mayflies. Common Usages: Often used to describe fleeting moments, temporary states, or transient phenomena.
